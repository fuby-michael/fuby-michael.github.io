ABOUT AKPAN DANIEL FOUNDATION( by the grace of God)

Building the young generation

The AKPAN DANIEL FOUNDATION is a non-governmental organization dedicated to inspiring and educating Nigeria's younger generation to care about the country's growth and development by educating them about the negative impacts of anti-social behaviors on both personal and national development, such as insecurity of life and property, unattractive destination for foreign investment and tourism etc, as well as encouraging them to take positive action and responsibility to curb them.

We're also dedicated to assisting those children who lack access to primary or secondary education, as well as any other children in need, to return to school.

WHAT WE DO FOR SCHOOLS

Supporting teachers and schools

Our free educational resources and workshops assist schools, instructors, and students to have a better understanding of real-world experience as well as the environmental difficulties that our beloved state and country face.

Class room resources

Every year, we provide teachers and our youngsters with school resources in order to engage and inspire them. Our collaboration with schools is essential to achieving our goal of guaranteeing a brighter future for our children and state.

Our motivational Education program

At the end of each year of our activity, we arrange a general summer reading and spelling challenge for our young people in order to foster reading culture among primary pupils and secondary students across our involvement schools, as well as to inspire and motivate them to do more.

ENTREPRENEURIAL EDUCATION

During short vacations, we provide entrepreneurial knowledge and skills acquisition to our secondary school students in fields such as baking, soap making, and shoe manufacturing, among other things, in order to develop and inspire our young people to become great entrepreneurs and mostly to be constantly engaged during the process
