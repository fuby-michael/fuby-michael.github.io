ABOUT AKPAN DANIEL FOUNDATION( by the grace of God)

Building the young generation

The AKPAN DANIEL FOUNDATION is a non-governmental organization dedicated to inspiring and educating Nigeria's younger generation to care about the country's growth and development by educating them about the negative impacts of anti-social behaviors on both personal and national development, such as insecurity of life and property, unattractive destination for foreign investment and tourism etc, as well as encouraging them to take positive action and responsibility to curb them. We're also dedicated to assisting those children who lack access to primary or secondary education, as well as any other children in need, to return to school.

We are confident that, with God's help, we will be able to promote a more sane society for future generations, a less conflict-filled world and a self-reliant society in the future if we enlighten, educate and sensitize our young people, instilling good moral values, and understanding that a society or nation is strong only when it cares for the weak, and wealthy only when it cares for the poor. As a result, we strive to instill in our youngsters a strong sense of social responsibility, a clear vision, and faith in our Stateâ€™s and country's common good.

Why the young people

Young people are the future of our country. For humanity's survival, we must empower young people with the skills, awareness, and practical skills to recognize the necessity of personal and social responsibility and education plays a critical role in preparing young people for the challenges that lie ahead and in securing the future. There is no greater accomplishment than preparing young people for the challenges that they will face in the future. There is no greater way to leave a legacy than to equip young people with the tools they need to preserve our environment and they can as well be shaped into any great figure we desired.

Our Mission

Our mission is to contribute to our Nation's development and prosperity, as well as the eradication of insecurity, by investing resources in the education of our younger generation.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a quality sustainable society in future.

Our approach

At ADF, we believe that respecting indigenous peoples' and local communities' rights, territory, laws, and culture is critical to achieving inclusive and sustainable development and finding the most effective solutions to the world's most serious environmental issues.

We are dedicated to operating in accordance with indigenous and local knowledge and advice, including collaborating with indigenous peoples and local communities when invited. Partnering with indigenous peoples and local communities values their expertise, sovereignty, governance, and leadership, all of which are critical for preserving the environment and restoring a valuable coexistence, as well as advancing economic stability and other community needs.

To ensure our safety and transparency, we've put in place a safeguarding structure. We manage risk, uphold human rights, and improve community and environmental outcomes through our programs. We use safeguards to identify, avoid, and limit any negative social and environmental effects of our activities while maximizing the positive outcomes.
